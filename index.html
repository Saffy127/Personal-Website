Project 1: MasterChiefGPT and CortanaGPT Discussions

GPTvsGPT: Halo Edition is an engaging Python application that simulates a conversation between two AI assistants, each embodying the unique personalities of Master Chief and Cortana from the Halo series. This playful program harnesses the power of OpenAI's Assistant API to create an intriguing dialogue, bringing the distinctive characteristics of these iconic characters to life. The conversation unfolds around a specified topic, showcasing how each assistant's traits influence their responses.
